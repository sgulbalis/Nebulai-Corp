# Identity & Verifiable Credentials

## Overview

Nebulai is built on the belief that identity is foundational to trust.

In today’s digital economy, identity is fragmented across platforms, institutions, and systems. Professional credibility is often inferred from resumes, platform profiles, and informal references rather than grounded in portable, verifiable evidence.

Nebulai introduces a more durable model of identity for the future of work and innovation.

This model is designed to enable:

- portable professional identity
- verifiable skills and credentials
- evidence-based reputation
- privacy-aware participation
- trusted collaboration across organizations and ecosystems

---

## The Identity Problem

Modern identity systems do not adequately support a distributed, project-based, AI-enabled economy.

### Fragmentation
People maintain multiple identities across work platforms, social networks, institutions, and organizations.

### Limited Portability
A reputation earned in one context often does not transfer to another.

### Weak Verification
Claims about skills, achievements, and contributions are frequently difficult to validate.

### Privacy Tradeoffs
Participants are often forced to reveal more personal information than necessary in order to establish trust.

### Platform Dependence
Identity and reputation are commonly controlled by centralized systems that users do not own.

---

## Nebulai’s Identity Model

Nebulai supports an identity model that is:

- participant-centered
- portable across contexts
- linked to verifiable evidence
- respectful of privacy and consent
- designed for long-term trust

This model connects identity not only to who a participant is, but also to:

- what they have done
- what has been verified
- what roles they have played
- how they have contributed over time

---

## Portable Identity

Nebulai is designed to support a portable identity layer that can persist across projects, institutions, and ecosystems.

A portable identity can connect:

- credentials
- project history
- contribution records
- trust and reputation signals
- role participation

This allows individuals and organizations to carry credibility with them rather than rebuilding it from scratch in every new environment.

---

## Verifiable Credentials

Nebulai emphasizes the importance of verifiable credentials as an alternative to self-reported claims.

These credentials may relate to:

- education and training
- certifications
- project outcomes
- institutional affiliations
- validated skills
- role-specific participation

The goal is to enable participants to present credible, portable proof of qualifications and contribution without relying solely on static documents or closed platforms.

---

## Evidence-Based Reputation

Identity alone is not enough.

Nebulai is designed to support a reputation model grounded in evidence, such as:

- validated deliverables
- successful completion of work
- peer and institutional attestations
- consistent participation over time
- quality of outcomes

This creates a stronger basis for trust than ratings or popularity metrics alone.

Reputation becomes more meaningful when it reflects documented contribution and verified performance.

---

## Roles and Identity

Nebulai supports participation across multiple roles.

These may include:

- creators
- validators
- curators
- mentors
- organizations
- institutions

A participant’s identity should be capable of reflecting these roles and the trust signals associated with each.

This enables more flexible and accurate representations of how people contribute across the ecosystem.

---

## Privacy-Aware Identity

Trust should not require unnecessary exposure.

Nebulai supports a privacy-aware approach where participants can prove what matters without sharing everything.

This includes principles such as:

- minimal disclosure
- selective sharing of credentials
- controlled visibility of identity data
- context-specific verification
- respect for participant consent

Privacy-aware identity helps reduce bias, protect dignity, and improve confidence in digital participation.

## Identity Across the Lifecycle of Work

Identity in Nebulai supports the full lifecycle of participation.

### Discovery

### Engagement

### Execution

This creates an identity system that grows with experience and verified contribution.
