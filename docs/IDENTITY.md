# Identity & Verifiable Credentials

## Overview

Nebulai establishes a new foundation for professional identity in the digital economy.

Traditional identity systems are fragmented, platform-dependent, and often unverifiable.  
Nebulai replaces these with a **portable, verifiable, and user-controlled identity model** built on open standards.

This enables individuals and organizations to:
- prove skills and contributions  
- build trusted reputation  
- collaborate across systems and institutions  
- maintain privacy and control over personal data  

---

## The Problem with Identity Today

Modern identity systems are not designed for the future of work.

### Fragmentation
Professionals maintain multiple profiles across platforms (LinkedIn, resumes, gig platforms), none of which are unified or interoperable.

### Lack of Verification
Credentials and experience are difficult to verify across organizations, leading to inefficiencies and risk.

### Platform Dependency
Identity and reputation are locked into centralized systems and cannot be easily transferred.

### Privacy Limitations
Users are often required to expose more data than necessary to prove qualifications.

---

## The Nebulai Identity Model

Nebulai introduces a **decentralized identity framework** that combines:

- Decentralized Identifiers (DIDs)  
- Verifiable Credentials (VCs)  
- Evidence-based reputation  
- Privacy-preserving verification mechanisms  

This creates a unified, portable identity layer for the global workforce.

---

## Core Components

### 1. Decentralized Identifiers (DIDs)

DIDs provide a unique, user-controlled identity.

#### Key Characteristics
- owned and controlled by the user  
- independent of any single platform  
- cryptographically verifiable  
- portable across systems  

DIDs act as the foundation for all identity, credentials, and reputation within Nebulai.

---

### 2. Verifiable Credentials (VCs)

Verifiable Credentials represent proof of skills, achievements, and contributions.

#### Examples
- completed projects  
- certifications and degrees  
- validated skills  
- performance outcomes  
- institutional attestations  

#### Key Properties
- cryptographically signed  
- tamper-resistant  
- issued by trusted entities or validated workflows  
- selectively shareable  

VCs replace static resumes with **dynamic, verifiable evidence**.

---

### 3. Evidence-Based Reputation

Reputation in Nebulai is derived from real, verifiable activity.

#### Inputs
- project completion and outcomes  
- validated deliverables  
- peer and validator attestations  
- consistency and reliability  
- contribution impact  

#### Characteristics
- non-transferable  
- resistant to manipulation  
- portable across contexts  
- continuously evolving  

Reputation reflects **what has been proven, not what is claimed**.

---

### 4. Non-Transferable Credentials (SBT-Style)

Nebulai may utilize non-transferable credentials (similar to Soulbound Tokens) to represent:

- reputation markers  
- role-based achievements  
- trust signals tied to identity  

#### Benefits
- cannot be sold or transferred  
- tied to actual identity and behavior  
- preserve long-term credibility  
- prevent gaming of the system  

---

### 5. Selective Disclosure & Privacy

Users control how their identity and credentials are shared.

#### Capabilities
- share only relevant information  
- prove qualifications without exposing full data  
- protect sensitive personal details  
- maintain compliance with privacy standards  

#### Technologies
- selective disclosure mechanisms  
- zero-knowledge proofs (where applicable)  
- encryption and secure storage  

This ensures:

> users can prove what matters without oversharing.

Identity is integrated into every stage of work within Nebulai.

### Discovery

- outcomes are reflected in reputation
