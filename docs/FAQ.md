# Frequently Asked Questions

## What is Nebulai?

Nebulai is a human-centered trust infrastructure for the future of work and innovation.

It is designed to support trusted coordination across people, institutions, intelligent systems, knowledge, and value exchange through frameworks centered on identity, contribution, governance, and accountable collaboration.

---

## Is Nebulai a marketplace?

Not in the narrow sense.

Nebulai may support marketplace-like interactions, but its public positioning is broader. It is designed as a coordination and trust framework rather than only a talent marketplace, job board, or transactional platform.

---

## Is Nebulai an AI platform?

Not only.

AI is an important part of Nebulai, but it is treated as a governed coordination capability within a broader system of trust, identity, contribution, governance, and value exchange.

Nebulai is not positioned as an AI product alone.

---

## Is Nebulai a Web3 project?

Nebulai incorporates Web3-aligned concepts such as decentralized identity, verifiable contribution, programmable agreements, and trust-aware coordination.

However, it is not positioned as a speculative crypto project. Its public framing is centered on practical trust infrastructure for work, innovation, and long-term ecosystem coordination.

---

## Is Nebulai open source?

No.

This repository is public-facing, but it is not an open-source implementation repository.

It is intended to share Nebulai’s public vision, architecture principles, governance model, and thought leadership while protecting proprietary implementation details.

For more information, see [REPOSITORY_SCOPE.md](./REPOSITORY_SCOPE.md).

---

## What is public in this repository?

This repository shares:

- public whitepaper summaries and narrative materials

These materials are intentionally strategic and conceptual.

## Who is Nebulai for?

Nebulai is relevant to a broad range of participants and stakeholders, including:

- independent professionals
- enterprises
- universities and research institutions
- public sector organizations
- innovation ecosystems
- ethical AI and Web3 communities
- strategic partners and investors

Its public documentation is intended to support understanding across these groups.

---

## What problem is Nebulai solving?

Nebulai addresses structural fragmentation in modern work and innovation systems.

This includes challenges such as:

- fragmented identity
- weak contribution visibility
- siloed knowledge
- trust-poor coordination
- opaque value exchange
- misalignment between effort and long-term value

Nebulai is designed to create a stronger trust layer across these conditions.

---

## What makes Nebulai different?

Nebulai’s public differentiation comes from the way it brings together:

- trust as infrastructure
- portable identity
- verifiable contribution
- governed AI
- milestone-based coordination
- knowledge and IP alignment
- broader synchronization of intelligence across people, AI, knowledge, and incentives

This gives Nebulai a broader strategic role than a single product category.

---

## What does “Synchronization of Intelligence” mean?

It refers to Nebulai’s public framing that intelligence in the modern economy is distributed across:

- people
- institutions
- AI systems
- intellectual property and knowledge
- capital and incentives

Nebulai is designed to support trust-based coordination across these elements so they can interact more effectively as one system.

For more detail, see [INTELLIGENCE_SYNCHRONIZATION.md](./INTELLIGENCE_SYNCHRONIZATION.md).

---

## What does “trust infrastructure” mean?

It means Nebulai is designed to make trust a system capability rather than an informal assumption.

This includes stronger foundations for:

- identity
- credentials
- contribution visibility
- agreements
- governance
- accountability
- transparent value flow

For more detail, see [TRUST_ENGINE.md](./TRUST_ENGINE.md).

---

## Why is identity so important to Nebulai?

Identity is foundational to trusted coordination.

Without stronger identity and credential models, contribution becomes difficult to verify, reputation remains fragmented, and trust does not travel well across projects, institutions, or borders.

For more detail, see [IDENTITY.md](./IDENTITY.md).

---

## Why does Nebulai emphasize human-centered AI?

Nebulai treats AI as a governed capability that should support people rather than replace agency, dignity, or accountability.

The platform’s public AI position emphasizes fairness, privacy, consent, transparency, and human oversight.

For more detail, see [AI_ETHICS.md](./AI_ETHICS.md).

---

## Why does Nebulai talk about intellectual property?

Because knowledge and intellectual contribution are central to innovation, yet they are often poorly coordinated, weakly attributed, and disconnected from long-term value participation.

Nebulai’s public framework extends beyond labor coordination into a broader model for trust, attribution, reuse, and innovation.

For more detail, see [IP_ECONOMY.md](./IP_ECONOMY.md).
